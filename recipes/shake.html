Protein Shake

Description

High protein shake perfect for a snack.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz almond milk
  • 2 scoops protein powder
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tbsp powdered peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp old-fashioned oats

Steps

  1. Pour 16 oz of almond milk into a blender
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients to the blender
  3. Blend until all ingredients have combined
