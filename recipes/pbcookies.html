Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Gluten-free, quick-and-easy peanut butter cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup sugar

Steps

  1. Mix egg, peanut butter and sugar in a mixing bowl
  2. Roll into ~1 inch balls
  3. Place balls onto a cookie sheet about 2-3 inches apart
  4. With a fork, make two perpendicular presses on top of each ball to flatten
  5. Bake at 350 degrees farenheit for 9-12 minutes or until golden brown
  6. Remove cookies from oven and let cook for 10-15 minutes
