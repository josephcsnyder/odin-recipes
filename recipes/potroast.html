Mississipi Pot Roast

Description

Easy to prepare and delicious pot roast recipe for the crock pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3-4 pounds) chuck roast
  • 1 packet ranch dressing mix
  • 1 packet au jus gravy mix
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 4-5 pepperoncini peppers

Steps

  1. Place roast in slow cooker
  2. Sprinkle ranch dressing mix and au jus mix over roast
  3. Place peppers on top of the mixes
  4. Add butter
  5. Cook on low heat for 8 hours
